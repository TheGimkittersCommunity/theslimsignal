Privacy Policy


Effective Date: 2/24/2026


Last Updated: 2/24/2026


Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.



1. Information We Collect

We collect specific personal information to provide and maintain our forum services:


  • Email Address: Required for account registration, security verification, and essential notifications.
  • Username and Nickname: Used to identify you on the forum and personalize your experience.
  • IP Address: Collected automatically when you access the forum for security purposes and to enforce site-wide bans.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our forum uses "cookies"—small text files stored on your device—to enhance your experience:


  • Essential Cookies: Required for the forum to function (e.g., keeping you logged in).
  • Functionality Cookies: Used to remember your preferences, such as your display theme.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our site.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is processed for the following reasons:


  • To manage your account and provide community features.
  • To maintain a safe environment by identifying and banning users who violate our terms (via IP tracking).
  • To analyze performance and improve site functionality.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. System logs and IP addresses are stored for a limited period to prevent abuse and ensure security.


5. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to:


  • Access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to the processing of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential cookies via your browser settings or our consent banner.
